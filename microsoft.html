About Microsoft

Microsoft Corporation is a multinational technology company based in Redmond, Washington, that manufactures computer software, consumer electronics, personal computers, and related services. The Microsoft Windows operating system, the Microsoft Office suite, and the Internet Explorer and Edge web browsers are among the company's most well-known software products.

Microsoft Recruitment Process

1. Recruiter Connect

It is highly recommended to maintain a good LinkedIn profile to get noticed by Microsoft recruiters. It is also advised to connect with existing Microsoft employees to get referrals for job positions.

2. Interview Rounds

The interview rounds start with an initial screening interview which is DS/Algo-based. The later rounds are more technical and focus on DS/Algo skills along with system design knowledge.
